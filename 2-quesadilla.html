The second recipe you will learn here is Quesadillas

  1. 2 Tortillas
  2. Cheese
  3. Some kind of protein (Ckicken, Beef, etc)
  4. Salt
  5. Smoked Pakrika
  6. Hot sauce
    7.

First, we will start with preparations.

  • Preheat you pan on low
  • Place the tortillas on a plate
  • Put you protein, cheese, seasonings, and hot sauce on the tortillas. (make sure you dont put too much on the tortilla)

These steps are simple. Place the tortilla with all of the ingredients on
the pan first, and place the other tortilla on top. Then let the first side
(tortilla) cook until it stays flat when you lift it with a spatula. Then
do the same for the other side.

After all of that, get the sides you want for your quesadilla and enjoy!

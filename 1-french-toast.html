The first recipe you will learn here is French Toast

  1. Bread (preferably thick)
  2. 4 Eggs (amount may vary)
  3. 2/3 Cup Milk
  4. 2 Tsp Cinnamon
  5. Butter
  6. Syrup
    7.

First, we will start with preparations.

  • Start by getting out a bowl.
  • Crack 4 eggs open and put the yolks into the bowl
  • Then mix in a 2/3 cup of milk and throw in 2 tsp of cinnamon and mix it in aswell.

After all of that, put a bit up butter in a pan and start heating it. While it heats up,
dip a slice of butter in the bowl then put it in the pan until it turns golden brown.
After one side is golden brown, flip the bread and cook the other side until its
golden brown aswell. Now repeat that process, putting a new slice of butter in the pan each time.

When you complete all of that, dress the french toast however you like, and enjoy!

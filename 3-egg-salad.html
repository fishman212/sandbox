The third and final recipe you will learn here is Egg Salad

  1. 4 or 5 eggs
  2. Mayo
  3. Mustard
  4. Salt
  5. Pepper
  6. Smoked Pakrika
  7. Lemon Juice
    8.

First, we will start with preparations.

  • Boil a pot of water
  • Get a bowl and fill it with cold water

After finishing preparations, gently place your eggs in the boiling water,
and boil them on medium/low for 10 - 12 minutes. Then gently set those
eggs in the cold water and let them cool for 3 - 5 minutes. Then peel the
shells off the eggs while running them under cold water. Place each finished
egg into a container/bowl. After all eggs are finished, place mayo and mustard
into the bowl/container they are in. Mash the mixture together, then add lemon juice,
salt, pepper, and smoked paprika. Then continue mixing.

And now, your recipe is finished. Add anything else you want, and enjoy!
